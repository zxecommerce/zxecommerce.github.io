    • 3. IMAGES & CONTENT Images that represent the SKU. Title, descriptions and meta-data that drives the internal search engine and describes the SKU. Also includes ratings and reviews.

    Service & Exp.

    • 4. Service Levels & Experience Supply chain and service considerations, including out of stock, delivery, product quality, packaging, technology improvements, returns, replacements substitution and regulatory issues. Delivery customer experience, including age verification, beer delivered cold, as well as ”surprise and delight” deliveries: free product, celebrity delivery, etc.
  • 5
    Navigation

    • 5. Navigation How shoppers navigate to/within beer, including taxonomy classification (Drinks > Beer > Lager) and layout of beer products
  • 6
    In-Site Search

    • 6. In-Site Search Search capabilities within the retailer site. Includes search word sponsorship for category searches (e.g. “lager”) and optimization of ABI brand searches (e.g. “Stella”).
  • 7
    In-Site Media

    • 7. In-Site Media Points of interruption (paid or free) across retailer site. Includes banners, cross merchandising, notifications and recommendations.
  • 8
    Off-Site Media

    Add a numbered list

    • 8. Off-Site Media Directing shoppers from offline or other online sites to points of purchase within the retailer’s sites. Includes social, external search, brand digital content, QR codes, emails, external URL linking and direct marketing.
  • 9
    Recurrence

    • 9. Recurrence Tools that strengthen the customer relationship and increase lifetime value, e.g. favorites & lists and subscription offerings.

    Price & Promo

    • 2. Pricing & Promotions Standard pricing and value-added and price promotions, including coupons, vouchers, PromOpti, badges on SKUs, special offers pages and loyalty/reward programs. Secondary displays (points of interruption) will often be used to show promotions and features.
